Can therapy help prevent suicides?

A new study shows that a brief course of up to 10 sessions of talk therapy compared with standard treatment reduced the risk of subsequent suicide attempts by up to 50% in people treated in hospital emergency rooms for attempted suicide.

The map on this page displays suicides per 100,000 people by country. Hovering over a country will provide the actual numbers and also list the number of mental health facilities in that country per 100,000 people (when available). While the data is largely inconclusive, many countries with low number of mental health facilities do see higher rates of suicide.
