Not feeling so great?

You have many avenues to connect with people who care. Here are a few:

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline

If you’re thinking about suicide, are worried about someone, or would like emotional support, the Lifeline network is available 24/7 by calling 800-273-8255 or via chat.

Guided Meditations from UCLA

Meditation is a mental exercise that trains attention and awareness. Its purpose is often to curb reactivity to one's negative thoughts and feelings. Start meditating today.

Trans Lifeline

Trans Lifeline provides trans peer support for our community that’s been divested from police since day one. The hotline is open 24/7 by calling 877-565-8860.

Online Therapy with BetterHelp.com

Making professional therapy accessible, affordable, and convenient — so anyone who struggles with life’s challenges can get help, anytime and anywhere. Get started.
